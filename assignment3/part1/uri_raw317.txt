			<h1 class="hdg hdg_h1 hdg_mainColor"><a href="https://www.profootballrumors.com/2017/02/patriots-jimmy-garoppolo-bill-belichick">Extra Points: Garoppolo, Belichick, Williams</a></h1>
		</div>
		<div class="post-author">
			<span>February 22nd, 2017 at 11:48am CST</span> by Zach Links 		</div>
	</div>
	<div class="post-bd">
		<div data-swiftype-index='true' class="userContent">
			<p>Three executives tell <a href="http://bleacherreport.com/articles/2693832-mike-freemans-10-point-stance-patriots-big-trade-bait-nfls-athletic-freaks" target="_blank">Mike Freeman of Bleacher Report</a> that they believe several teams will be making blockbuster offers for <strong>Patriots</strong> quarterback <strong><a href="http://www.pro-football-reference.com/players/G/GaroJi00.htm?utm_campaign=Linker&amp;utm_source=direct&amp;utm_medium=linker-" target="_blank" rel="nofollow">Jimmy Garoppolo</a></strong>. Ultimately, they think that will result in an offer the Pats cannot refuse.</p>
<p>Freeman also hears from Patriots sources that <strong>Bill Belichick</strong> likely plans to stay on as coach for at least five more years. Those same sources believe that Belichick wants to continue coaching after Brady retires to show that he can win without the best quarterback of all time.</p>
<p>Here&#8217;s more from around the NFL:</p>
<ul>
<li><strong><a href="http://www.pro-football-reference.com/players/B/BennAr00.htm?utm_campaign=Linker&amp;utm_source=direct&amp;utm_medium=linker-" target="_blank" rel="nofollow">Arrelious Benn</a></strong>&#8216;s <a href="https://www.profootballrumors.com/2017/02/jaguars-re-sign-wr-arrelious-benn" target="_blank">new contract</a> with the <strong>Jaguars</strong> is a two-year, $1.765MM deal that contains $100K guaranteed, <a href="https://twitter.com/caplannfl/status/834115464175427586" target="_blank">tweets</a> Adam Caplan of ESPN.com. Although he didn&#8217;t contribute much to the Jacksonville offense (just five receptions for 116 yards), Benn was a regular on special teams duty, as he saw action on 63% of the Jaguars&#8217; ST plays.</li>
<li>Cornerback <strong><a href="http://www.pro-football-reference.com/players/W/WillKW00.htm?utm_campaign=Linker&amp;utm_source=direct&amp;utm_medium=linker-" target="_blank" rel="nofollow">K&#8217;Waun Williams</a></strong>&#8216; grievance against the <strong>Browns</strong> will be heard on May 16, reports <a href="http://www.cleveland.com/browns/index.ssf/2017/02/kwaun_williams_grievance_again.html" target="_blank">Mary Kay Cabot of Cleveland.com</a>. Williams, who played for Cleveland from 2014-15, contends the Browns waived him while he was <a href="https://www.profootballrumors.com/2016/09/north-notes-k-williams-browns-ansah-lions-bears" target="_blank">still dealing with an injured ankle</a>. As such, Williams is seeking the entirety of his 2016 base salary ($600K), plus the cost of his surgery.</li>
<li>Tennessee running back <strong><a href="http://www.sports-reference.com/cfb/players/alvin-kamara-1.html?utm_campaign=Linker&amp;utm_source=direct&amp;utm_medium=linker-" target="_blank" rel="nofollow">Alvin Kamara</a></strong> has fired VaynerSports and hired Damarius Bilbo of Revolution Sports Group as his new representation , according to Liz Mullen of SportsBusiness Journal (<a href="https://twitter.com/SBJLizMullen/status/834085477439270912" target="_blank">Twitter</a> <a href="https://twitter.com/SBJLizMullen/status/834173076757622785" target="_blank">links</a>). Kamara, a committee back with the Volunteers, managed less than 1,300 yards rushing during two years in Tennessee, but also averaged more than six yards per carry during that time. Largely viewed as a Day 2 prospect, recent buzz has indicated Kamara could sneak into Round 1.</li>
</ul>
<p><em>Dallas Robinson contributed to this post.</em></p>
