Fine Seafood at its Best

“The fish could only be fresher if it swam in by itself.”
- The New York Times

   Hugo’s Frog Bar & Fish House opened its doors next to the renowned
   Gibsons Bar & Steakhouse, in February of 1997. After operating
   Chicago’s best steakhouse for six years, partners Steve Lombardo and
   Hugo Ralli decided to open the city’s finest seafood restaurant.

   Hugo's receives its seafood daily. It is Chicago’s foremost spot for
   fresh oysters, crudos, frog legs, lobster and fish, plus [20]USDA
   Gibsons Prime Angus steaks and chops. Hugo’s boasts extensive wine and
   cocktail lists, and impressive signature desserts.
