</p><p>More than 17 years removed from his final NFL pass and 12 years after his enshrinement in the Pro Football Hall of Fame, Marino &quot;re-signed&quot; with the Dolphins on Wednesday &mdash; as were five other prominent former members of the team, all inking ceremonial one-day contracts designed so they could officially retire as members of the franchise.</p><p>&quot;This organization, the team, the city, football, it all means so much to me,&quot; Marino said. &quot;It&#39;s been my first love.
