    <h1>Tom Brady&#8217;s Missing Jersey: Conspiracy Theory</h1>
	<div class="post_meta">
    <span class="author_avatar"><a href="http://949whom.com/author/nikkicruz/" title="Nikki Cruz"><img src="http://wac.450F.edgecastcdn.net/80450F/949whom.com/files/2016/12/nikki.jpg?w=40&amp;h=40&amp;zc=1&amp;s=0&amp;a=t&amp;q=89" /></a></span>
    <span class="the_author"><span>By </span><a href="http://949whom.com/author/nikkicruz/" title="Nikki Cruz">Nikki Cruz</a></span>
    <span class="the_date">February 22, 2017 8:02 PM</span>    </div>	<div class="social-group-top large"><div class="social_icons size_large round">

	<a   class="facebook share_link" href="http://www.facebook.com/sharer.php?u=http%3A%2F%2F949whom.com%2Ftom-bradys-missing-jersey-conspiracy-theory%2F%3Ftrackback%3Dfbshare_top&t=Tom+Brady%26%238217%3Bs+Missing+Jersey%3A+Conspiracy+Theory" title="Share on Facebook" target="_blank">

	<a   class="twitter share_link" href="http://twitter.com/intent/tweet?text=Tom%20Brady%E2%80%99s%20Missing%20Jersey%3A%20Conspiracy%20Theory%20http%3A%2F%2F949whom.com%2Ftom-bradys-missing-jersey-conspiracy-theory%2F%3Ftrackback%3Dtwitter_top%20%40949WHOM" title="Share on Twitter" target="_blank">

        <figure id="attachment_144412" class="content-post-image alignnone" ><img src="http://wac.450F.edgecastcdn.net/80450F/949whom.com/files/2017/02/Tom-Brady-Stolen-Super-Bowl-Jersey-Getty-Images-634604302.jpg?w=630&#038;h=420&#038;zc=1&#038;s=0&#038;a=t&#038;q=89" alt="Super Bowl LI - New England Patriots v Atlanta Falcons" width="630" height="420" class="size-630scaled wp-image-144412" /><figcaption style="width:630px;">(Photo By Kevin C. Cox/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>Now that the Houston Police Department have released the estimated value of Tom Brady&#8217;s Super Bowl game jersey, you can bet the black market value has probably doubled.</p>
<p>Here&#8217;s the police report:</p>
<blockquote class="twitter-tweet" data-lang="en"><p lang="en" dir="ltr">NEW: Houston PD release police report taken on Tom Brady&#39;s missing/stolen jersey. Estimated value: $500,000 <a href="https://twitter.com/hashtag/wbz?src=hash">#wbz</a> <a href="https://twitter.com/hashtag/Patriots?src=hash">#Patriots</a> <a href="https://twitter.com/hashtag/SuperBowl?src=hash">#SuperBowl</a> <a href="https://t.co/Tik4EqTBVg">pic.twitter.com/Tik4EqTBVg</a></p>
<p>&mdash; Jim Harrington (@jejharrington) <a href="https://twitter.com/jejharrington/status/834070257727848448">February 21, 2017</a></p></blockquote>
<p> <script async src="http://wac.450F.edgecastcdn.net/80450F/949whom.com/platform.twitter.com/widgets.js" charset="utf-8"></script></p>
<p>In a sense, there&#8217;s also a price on the thief&#8217;s head as the charge for this alleged crime is a first degree felony. There&#8217;s up to a $10,000 fine and possible imprisonment for &#8220;any term of not more than 99 years or less than 5 years.&#8221;</p>
<p>Most people assume an unscrupulous individual took it from Brady&#8217;s locker after the New England Patriot&#8217;s historic win over the Atlanta Falcons on February 5th. But, what if the jersey was never stolen? And if it was, it had to be an inside job or perhaps even a member of the media. Someone could be playing a game of &#8220;wag the dog&#8221; for reasons unknown to this writer. The media hype has been sensational and that alone is worth its weight in gold.</p>
<p>Whatever the case may be, this could become the biggest &#8220;who done it&#8221; mystery in U.S. history&#8230; bigger than the &#8220;who shot JR&#8221; scandal of 1980. That drama also unfolded in Texas. Has anyone checked Kristin Shepard&#8217;s (Mary Crosby) whereabouts on the night in question?</p>
