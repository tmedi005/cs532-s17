   VIP Championship Banner Raising Charity Raffle

   The New England Patriots Charitable Foundation Presents

   Championship Banner Raising Raffle

   Drawing to be held on Tuesday, May 2, 2017. The winner will be
   contacted by phone and/or email that day.

   Official Prize Package:
     * Four (4) – Lower Bowl Tickets
     * Four (4) – VIP Tailgate Passes
     * Four (4) – Pre-Game Field Passes
     * One (1) – VIP Parking Pass

   Eligibility

   Raffle only open to individual legal residents of the fifty (50) United
   States (excluding CA, FL, NY, Puerto Rico, territories and possessions)
   who have attained the age of majority in their state of residence as of
   the date of entry into the Raffle.

   Raffle Proceeds

   100% of proceeds from the Raffle will go to the New England Patriots
   Charitable Foundation, a 501(c)(3) not for profit corporation,
   supporting programs that aid New England youth and families by
   assisting programs that foster cultural diversity, education, family
   and health.

   Tickets

   Raffle tickets are $10 each. Please print your receipt as your official
   raffle ticket(s). Email [15]CharitableEvents@patriots.com with any
   questions or concerns. No refunds on ticket purchases. Winners must be
   at least 18 years of age. Drawing will take place Tuesday, May 2, 2017.
   Winner will be notified by phone or email.
