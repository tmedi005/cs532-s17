<div class="byline">
By <em><a href="/users/jasmine-watkins" title="View user profile." class="username">Jasmine Watkins</a></em>, 2/22/17, 1:09 PM EST </div>
<div class="article-image"><a href="tom-brady-suspect-list-missing-jersey-new-england-patriots-julian-edelman-lady-gaga"><img src="/sites/athlonsports.com/files/styles/article_top_img/public/xtb_1.jpg,qitok=lSoGf-tl.pagespeed.ic.48CFHkN1T1.jpg" alt="" data-pagespeed-url-hash="3319606681" onload="pagespeed.CriticalImages.checkImageForCriticality(this);"/></a>

<h3 class="subtitle">Everyone from Edelman to Lady Gaga is considered a suspect.</h3>
<div class="content clearfix">
<div class="field field-name-body field-type-text-with-summary field-label-hidden"><div class="field-items"><div class="field-item even"> <p>Tom Brady&#39;s jersey went missing shortly after the Super Bowl and he&#39;s been on a mission to find it ever since.</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>The Patriots quarterback takes a light-hearted approach to putting together a list of people who could&#39;ve taken the prized possession, valued&nbsp;by some at around $500 million. Everyone from Julian Edelman to Lady Gaga and even Scooby the dog could&#39;ve had a hand (or paw) in the crime.&nbsp;</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<blockquote class="instagram-media" data-instgrm-captioned="" data-instgrm-version="7" style=" background:#FFF; border:0; border-radius:3px; box-shadow:0 0 1px 0 rgba(0,0,0,0.5),0 1px 10px 0 rgba(0,0,0,0.15); margin: 1px; max-width:658px; padding:0; width:99.375%; width:-webkit-calc(100% - 2px); width:calc(100% - 2px);">
