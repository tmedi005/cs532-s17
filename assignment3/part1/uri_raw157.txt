<div class="newsBody"><p><strong>Williamsburg, Ky</strong>. - <a href="http://www.CumberlandsPatriots.com/roster/19/6/13361.php" class="rosterProfileLink  Male MGL">Ben Kendrick</a> (JR/Danville, KY) is a junior biology/pre-med major from Danville, Ky.&nbsp; This fall Kendrick won the Bill Sergent Invite shooting a two-day score of 144, and had four other top 10 finishes. &nbsp;He also earned MSC All- Conference Honors and MSC All- Academic Honors last season.&nbsp;</p><p>Kendrick came to UC because he wanted to go to an established program where he could win while being surrounded by great players and coaches that would make him a better player. &nbsp;His favorite class at UC is Physics 1 and his favorite professors are Dr. Tan and Dr. Dzugan.</p><p>His favorite golfer is Rory McIlroy, though his dream foursome would be his dad, granddad, and his roommate <a href="http://www.CumberlandsPatriots.com/roster/19/6/13365.php" class="rosterProfileLink  Male MGL">Ross Perry</a> (JR/Somerset, KY) at Danville Country Club. &nbsp;As far as favorite non-golfer athlete Kendrick says it&#39;s Roger Federer.</p><p>Kendrick&#39;s favorite memory at UC is winning the MSC conference championship last season. &nbsp;His favorite thing to do on campus is eat buffalo chicken wraps from the grill.</p><p>When he is not playing golf Kendrick likes to play ping-pong, play basketball, to workout and to read. You can also catch him watching his favorite movies Good Will Hunting, Star Wars, or Harry Potter, or watching his favorite TV Show M.A.S.H</p><p>You can follow Kendrick on Twitter (@bendrick18) and Instagram (@bendrick_18).</p><p>For the latest and most up-to-date information on University of the Cumberlands Athletics, please visit www.cumberlandspatriots.com.
