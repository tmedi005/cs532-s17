<p>It&#8217;s been set for months that pop star Lady Gaga will be performing during halftime on Sunday&#8217;s Super Bowl broadcast (Feb. 5). However, several people are hoping that another group of chart-toppers will take Mother Monster&#8217;s place during the telecast.</p>
<p class="speedbump"><strong>READ: <a href="http://www.vibe.com/2017/01/big-sean-i-decided-tracklist/" target="_blank">Eminem, Migos, &amp; Jhene Aiko All Featured On Big Sean’s ‘I Decided’</a></strong></p>
<p>A <a href="https://www.change.org/p/nfl-replace-lady-gaga-with-the-migos-for-superbowl-li-halftime-show" target="_blank">petition</a> to have the Migos take Lady Gaga&#8217;s performance slot has made the rounds online, with over 60,000 signatures. The &#8220;Bad And Boujee&#8221; hit-makers, whose album<em> Culture</em> is poised to be number-one on the charts, have caught wind of the petition, and are certainly down to make an appearance.</p>
<p>When asked by <a href="http://www.tmz.com/2017/02/01/migos-super-bowl-halftime-gaga/" target="_blank">TMZ</a>&#8217;s reporters Wednesday (Feb. 1) about the possibility of an appearance during the coveted halftime performance, the trio seemed gung-ho about joining the &#8220;Million Reasons&#8221; songbird, of course, if they&#8217;re asked by Gaga herself.</p>
<p>&#8220;Ay man, who knows, man. Whenever the phone call [comes],” said Quavo, while Offset replied with “Dat way.”</p>
<p>&#8220;We ain&#8217;t tryna take Lady Gaga off, we just wanna slide in first real quick,&#8221; said Takeoff. &#8220;Let us get in there.&#8221;</p>
<p>When another TMZ reporter asked if anyone from the NFL or Gaga&#8217;s team has reached out to them, Quavo started plugging the <em>Culture</em> album, jumping around the question entirely and neither confirming nor denying any news.</p>
<p>It would be fitting indeed to have the Georgia natives make an appearance, as the Atlanta Falcons are one of the teams playing for a Super Bowl ring (against the New England Patriots).</p>

<p>&#8220;Halftime should be Migos killing Bad and Boujee, Future kills em with March Madness, then Outkast comes out and does an AQUEMINI sampler and then they end it with BUN B and JAY Z DOING BIG PIMPIN!!!!&#8221; reads the petition. &#8220;If you&#8217;re gonna put on a show for billions THIS IS THE ONLY SHOW.&#8221;</p>
