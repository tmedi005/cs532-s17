That little detail is <a href="https://www.propublica.org/article/trump-plan-deport-anyone-crossing-mexican-border-regardless-of-nationality" target="_blank">buried deep</a> in the Trump administration&#8217;s plan to round up undocumented immigrants.</p>
<p>It&#8217;s as if Trump wants a war <a href="https://www.propublica.org/article/trump-plan-deport-anyone-crossing-mexican-border-regardless-of-nationality" target="_blank">with Mexico</a>:</p>
<blockquote><p>If present immigration trends continue, that could mean the United States would push hundreds of thousands of Guatemalans, Hondurans, Salvadorans, Brazilians, Ecuadorians, even Haitians into Mexico. Currently, such people are detained in the U.S. and allowed to request asylum.</p></blockquote>
<p>Meanwhile, Eric Trump has <a href="http://thehill.com/homenews/news/320043-trump-winery-seeks-more-foreign-help-report" target="_blank">requested permission </a>for 23 foreign workers to work for his Virginia winery. Those workers are to plant and harvest grapes for his business. This, following Donald Trump&#8217;s promise to bring back jobs for U.S. citizens.</p>
<p>Still left at the Statue of Liberty&#8217;s base is a plaque which reads, &#8220;Give me your tired, your poor, your huddled masses yearning to breathe free, the wretched refuse of your teeming shore. Send these, the homeless, tempest-tossed to me, I lift my lamp beside the golden door!&#8221;</p>
