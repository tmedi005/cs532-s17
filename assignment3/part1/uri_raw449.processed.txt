Super Bowl 52 odds for the Oakland Raiders.

   The Oakland Raiders had a great year in 2016, but aren’t quite at the
   top of the sportsbooks as far as Super Bowl 52 odds are concerned.

   Per [35]OddsShark, the Oakland Raiders, including ties, have the
   seventh best odds to win Super Bowl 52, which will be played in the
   Minnesota Vikings’ new stadium in Minneapolis.
   New England Patriots +450
   Dallas Cowboys       +1000
   Green Bay Packers    +1100
   Seattle Seahawks     +1100
   Atlanta Falcons      +1250
   Pittsburgh Steelers  +1300
   Denver Broncos       +1600
   Kansas City Chiefs   +1900
   Oakland Raiders      +1900
   New York Giants      +1900
   Indianapolis Colts   +2750
   Carolina Panthers    +2800
   Minnesota Vikings    +2800

   Miami Dolphins       +4700
   Tennessee Titans     +4700
   Washington Redskins  +5500
   Cincinnati Bengals   +5500
   Detroit Lions        +6000
   New Orleans Saints   +6600
   Los Angeles Chargers +7000
   Buffalo Bills        +7500
   Jacksonville Jaguars +8500
   Chicago Bears        +10000
   Los Angeles Rams     +11000
   New York Jets        +11500
   San Francisco 49ers  +22500
   Cleveland Browns     +22500


   Among AFC teams, OddsShark has the Patriots, Steelers, and Broncos all
   as having better odds to win Super Bowl 52, with the Chiefs having the
   same as Oakland.

   [36]The Westgate has the Raiders tied for the fifth best mark, at 20-1.

   They have the Patriots leading the way at 6-1, the Cowboys and Steelers
   tied at 10-1, Packers and Seahawks tied at 12-1, and then the Falcons
   just before the Raiders at 16-1.
   [37]Next: Potential Cap Casualties

   The Panthers, Broncos and Chiefs are all to 20-1, tied with Oakland.

   What are your thoughts on the odds? Are the Raiders too high, too low,
   or just right?
