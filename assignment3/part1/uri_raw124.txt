<p><strong>BOSTON &#8212;</strong> <a href="http://www.cbssports.com/nfl/teams/page/NE/new-england-patriots" target="_blank">New England Patriots</a> head coach Bill Belichick could be called as a witness in the double murder trial of <a href="http://www.cbsnews.com/news/aaron-hernandez-case-survivor-raychides-sanches-says-shooter-in-2012-boston-killings-looked-like/" target="_blank">Aaron Hernandez</a>, according to court documents released Wednesday,<a href="http://boston.cbslocal.com/2017/02/22/patriots-bill-belichick-aaron-hernandez-witness-murder-trial/" target="_blank"> CBS Boston reports</a>.</p>
<p>Belichick’s name appeared on a list of possible witnesses filed Tuesday by Hernandez’s defense.</p>
<p>Hernandez played for Belichick and the Patriots from 2010 to 2013. The former star tight end is being tried in <a href="http://www.cbsnews.com/news/aaron-hernandez-indicted-in-2012-boston-double-murder/" target="_blank">the drive-by slayings</a> of 29-year-old Daniel de Abreu and 28-year-old Safiro Furtado. Both men were shot to death as they left a Boston nightclub back in 2012. Prosecutors say Hernandez shot them after one spilled a drink on him at a club. Hernandez has pleaded not guilty. </p>
<p>There are dozens of potential civilian and expert witnesses listed on the new defense filing. “William Belichick, Hingham, MA” is No. 11. </p>
<p>Earlier in the month, a defense motion said Patriots offensive coordinator Josh McDaniels may testify on the possible meanings of some of Hernandez’s tattoos. </p>
<p>WBZ-TV Sports Director Steve Burton is also listed as a potential witness in the court paperwork. The documents cite Steve’s 2011 interview with Hernandez. </p>
<p>Jury selection is underway. Opening statements are scheduled March 1. </p>
<p>Hernandez grew up in Connecticut. He is serving a life sentence in the 2013 <a href="http://www.cbsnews.com/news/aaron-hernandez-guilty-of-first-degree-murder-in-death-of-odin-lloyd/" target="_blank">killing of Odin Lloyd, </a>who was dating the sister of Hernandez’s fiancee.</p>
