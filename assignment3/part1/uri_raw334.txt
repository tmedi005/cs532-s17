      <section class="desktop-section"> 
       <h2 class="uppercase" itemprop="headline">New England Patriots Heavily Favored to Re-Sign LeGarrette Blount</h2> 
       <p itemprop="description">The New England Patriots are clear-cut favorites to re-sign RB LeGarrette Blount, and experts question the impact he'd have on another team.</p> 
       <span itemprop="publisher" itemscope itemtype="http://schema.org/Organization"> 
        <meta itemprop="name" content="sportsline.com"> 
        <meta itemprop="url" content="http://www.sportsline.com"> 
        <meta itemprop="logo" content="http://www.sportsline.com/favicon.ico"> </span> 
       <meta itemprop="name" content="25780403detail"> 
       <section class="author-info" itemprop="author" itemscope itemtype="http://schema.org/Person"> 
        <img src="https://sports.cbsimg.net/images/sportsline/authors/50_clear_daniel_cypra.png"> 
        <div class="container"> 
         <div class="top"> 
          <div class="author-name darkGrayText"> 
           <span class="mobile">By </span> 
           <span itemprop="givenName">Daniel</span> 
           <span itemprop="familyName">Cypra</span>

     <section class="body-card"> 
      <section class="body"> 
       <span itemprop="articleBody"> <p>&nbsp;</p> 
        <figure> 
        </figure> 
        <div id="embedVideoContainer_113536566864" class="embedVideo">
         <iframe id="embedVideo_113536566864" name="embedVideo_113536566864" src="http://www.sportsline.com/video/player/embed?args=player_id%3D113536566864%26channel%3Dnfl%26pcid%3D879840323524%26width%3D640%26height%3D360%26autoplay%3Dtrue%26comp_ads_enabled%3Dfalse%26uvpc%3Dhttp%253A%252F%252Fsports.cbsimg.net%252Fvideo%252Fuvp%252Fconfig%252Fv3%252Fuvp_sportsline.xml%26uvpc_m%3Dhttp%253A%252F%252Fsports.cbsimg.net%252Fvideo%252Fuvp%252Fconfig%252Fv3%252Fuvp_sportsline_m.xml%26partner%3Dsportsline%26partner_m%3Dsportsline_mobile%26resizable%3Dtrue%26domain%3Dsportsline.com%26lazy_load%3Dtrue" width="640" height="360" marginwidth="0" marginheight="0" scrolling="no" frameborder="0" allowtransparency="true" allowfullscreen="true"></iframe>
        </div> <p><strong>New England Patriots</strong> running back LeGarrette Blount signed a one-year deal entering the 2016 season worth $1 million.</p> <p>The result: Blount led the league with 18 rushing touchdowns, two more than the next closest player and the most in Patriots history.</p> <p>Now, the Patriots have a decision to make: Sign Blount to a long-term deal, hit him with the franchise tag, or let him walk in free agency.</p> <p>Blount is a massive favorite to stay with the Patriots . According to odds posted at BookMaker.eu, he's -250 to play in Foxborough in 2017, meaning you'd have to wager $250 just to win $100.</p> <p><a href="http://www.cbssports.com/nfl/news/legarrette-blount-extremely-grateful-to-jeff-fisher-and-titans/" target="_blank"><strong>CBS SPORTS: BLOUNT GRATEFUL TO JEFF FISHER</strong></a></p> <p>Blount scored in all but two of the Patriots' regular-season games as New England went 14-2. He had six games with at least 20 carries and gave the Pats a bruising dimension on offense.</p> <p>"Blount knows he won't find a better situation than New England, and quite likely will take less money to stay," SportsLine's <a href="http://www.sportsline.com/experts/13773/larry-hartstein/">Larry Hartstein</a> said. "He'll be back to team with Dion Lewis and James White in a very deep, dynamic backfield."</p> <p>Blount just turned 30 and it's questionable whether he could command a high-dollar contract.</p> <p><iframe class="newsletter-signup embed" src="/sportsline-web/newsletter-signup-iframe.html"></iframe></p> <p>"You play this game to win, and that's all they do here is win football games and win championships," Blount told "The Rich Eisen Show."</p> <p>Also questionable is the impact he'd have on another team. Legendary Vegas oddsmaker <a href="http://www.sportsline.com/experts/33128071/kenny-white/">Kenny White</a>&nbsp;said Blount would make "zero difference" if he ended up on another team.</p> <p><a href="http://www.sportsline.com/experts/15770/zack-cimini/">Zack Cimini</a> agreed, saying New England "maximized his value." Both would take any other team besides the Patriots or the ones below landing Blount for Week 1.</p> <p>The <strong>Oakland Raiders</strong> are +500, or 5/1, to land Blount. Oakland could move on from RB Latavius Murray and try to attract Blount to complement RB DeAndre Washington.</p> <p>The <strong>New York Giants</strong>, who just cut RB Rashad Jennings, are also in the market for a back. Whether Blount would be a fit with Giants RB Paul Perkins (21 rushes for 102 yards in Week 17) remains to be seen.
