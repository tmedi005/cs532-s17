<h4 style="text-align: center;">On any street in nearly every urban neighborhood in America, you can find the local corner store &#8211; a one-stop shop and gathering point for the residents in that community. Each person takes pride in their local corner store and, even if they move away at some point, they always make their way back for visits to reminisce and reflect on where they grew up.</h4>
<h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong><em>Corner Store</em></strong> was established in 2016 with a vision to renew a sense of togetherness within the city of Atlanta. Our mission is to establish community pride through initiatives directly aimed at bettering the very neighborhoods that corner stores thrive in. As we grow and strengthen our ties to the streets and within the city, <em><strong>Corner Store</strong></em> plans to not only give Atlanta something to be proud of but show that charity truly begins at home.</h4>
