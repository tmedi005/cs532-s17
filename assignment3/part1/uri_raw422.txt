<p><span class="story_dl">Feb. 22 (UPI) --</span> It's an abnormal offseason for the <a href="http://www.upi.com/topic/New_England_Patriots/" title="New England Patriots" class="tpstyle">New England Patriots</a> when one player is headed to jail and coaches might testify in a double-murder trial.</p>
<p><a href="http://www.upi.com/topic/Bill_Belichick/" title="Bill Belichick" class="tpstyle">Bill Belichick</a> and <a href="http://www.upi.com/topic/Josh_McDaniels/" title="Josh McDaniels" class="tpstyle">Josh McDaniels</a> could be involved in <a href="http://www.upi.com/topic/Aaron_Hernandez/" title="Aaron Hernandez" class="tpstyle">Aaron Hernandez</a>'s double murder case, <a class="tpstyle" href="https://www.bostonglobe.com/metro/2017/02/22/aaron-hernandez-juror-looking-for-something/TicTLL7ZlGb60yixassOgJ/story.html" target="_blank" title="Globe on Patriots">according to the <em>Boston Globe</em></a>. Wide receiver Michael Floyd was recently <a class="tpstyle" href="http://www.upi.com/Sports_News/NFL/2017/02/17/New-England-Patriots-Michael-Floyd-sentenced-to-four-months-in-jail/3241487366954/" target="_blank" title="UPI on Hernandez">sentenced to four months in jail</a> for a December DUI.<div align="center"> <script type="text/javascript"><!--

<p>The former Patriots tight end pleaded not guilty to shooting and killing two men in 2012. He is already serving a life sentence for killing Odin Lloyd in 2013.</p>
<p>Patriots owner <a href="http://www.upi.com/topic/Robert_Kraft/" title="Robert Kraft" class="tpstyle">Robert Kraft</a> testified in the Lloyd trial, while Belichick was also on the witness list.</p>
<p>During his testimony, Kraft told the judge that Hernandez <a class="tpstyle" href="http://www.si.com/nfl/2015/03/31/aaron-hernandez-trial-robert-kraft-testifies" target="_blank" title="SI on Hernandez">told him he was innocent</a>.</p>
<p>Hernandez, 27, is charged with killing Daniel de Abreu and Safiro Furtado in 2012. The duo was killed in an alleged drive-by shooting.</p>
<p>Opening statements for the case are set for March 1. Hernandez played in 38 games for the Patriots, before being released.</p>
