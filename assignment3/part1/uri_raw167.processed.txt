Patriots Hype Video Will Send Chills Down Your Spine

   Patriots Hype Video Will Send Chills Down Your Spine
   [19]Tweet
   [20]Email
     * Dan Russo
     *
     * 4:29 pm, January 13th, 2017

   As far as hype videos go, this might be the most intimidating one I’ve
   ever seen.

   Even if you are a New England and Tom Brady hater this Patriots hype
   video will send chills down your spine.

     “I offered you… peace”

     Patriots 2016

     On To Houston [21]#OneMore [22]#DoYourJob
     [23]pic.twitter.com/lBFt8fjoHq

     — Drew (@HawaiianGronk) [24]January 12, 2017

   The opening monologue clearly hints that the NFL started a war by
   handing Brady a four-game suspension at the beginning of this season.
   Now, the four-time Super Bowl champion and Bill Belichick are here to
   finish the battle.

   The ultimate retribution for Brady and all of Pats nation would be for
   the quarterback to bring home his fifth Super Bowl ring. That starts
   with a win over the Texans in this Saturday’s divisional round game.

   While Jadeveon Clowney and the Texans defense should cause some
   problems for the Patriots offense, Brady has looked like a man on a
   mission this season, throwing for 28 touchdowns and just two
   interceptions in the 12 games he’s played.

   The only way the Texans may be able to come out with a victory is if
   Tom Brady gets outed as a Terminator. Since I don’t see that scenario
   playing out, be afraid Texans fans be very afraid.
