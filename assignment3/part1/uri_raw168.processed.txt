Tom Brady begins his own jersey investigation

   [19]Tom Brady cares a lot less about the whereabouts of his missing
   [20]Super Bowl jersey than initially believed.

   The [21]Patriots quarterback -- whose stolen uniform top was recently
   [22]valued by authorities at $500,000 -- took to Instagram on Wednesday
   to display a list of possible suspects in the case.

   [23]Hey @edelman11 "Ya suspect, yeah you! I don't know what your
   reputation is in this town, but after that s@?# you pulled, you can bet
   l'll be looking into you!"

   A post shared by TomBrady (@tombrady) on Feb 22, 2017 at 8:39am PST

   The pop culture references are flying fast and furious here: Lord Of
   The Rings, Billy Madison, Lady Gaga, Jaws, The Office, even Air Bud.
   The caption is a deep cut reference from Good Will Hunting, which will
   undoubtedly please the thousands of Sullys in Brady's fanbase. There's
   even a nod to the infamous Brady courtroom sketch from Deflategate.

   Of course, the only real lead produced by this "evidence board" is
   further proof that [24]Tom Brady employs a covert cadre of
   well-compensated social media specialists to do his bidding.

   Their gargantuan task: Make [25]Tom Brady Seem Personable And Maybe
   Funny At Times. This quality entry should land the entire team
   high-octane kale slurpees come bonus time.
