   Charles Krauthammer is one of the most respected conservative political
   commentators and thinkers. Check out what he said concerning the
   Iranian ransom situation!

   He said in relation to the $400 million payment we made to Iran for
   releasing prisoners: “They had to print the money here, ship it over to
   Switzerland, turn it into Swiss Francs and Euros, and then ship it over
   to Iran.”

   “If a private company had done this, this is called money laundering.
   The CEO would be in jail right now.”

   Krauthammer was reacting to a Wall Street Journal report saying that
   the Justice Department objected to the State Department’s decision to
   do this.
