	<header class="entry-header clearfix">
		<h1 class="entry-title">Browns One of Two Teams Reportedly Still Leading the Pack to Land Patriots QB Garoppolo</h1>
		<div class="entry-tags clearfix"><span>TOPICS:</span><a href="http://www.neosportsinsiders.com/tag/bears/" rel="tag">Bears</a><a href="http://www.neosportsinsiders.com/tag/browns/" rel="tag">Browns</a><a href="http://www.neosportsinsiders.com/tag/jimmy-garoppolo/" rel="tag">Jimmy Garoppolo</a></div>	</header>
<div class="entry-thumbnail">
<img width="594" height="396" src="http://www.neosportsinsiders.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/02/633479580.jpg" class="attachment-content-single size-content-single wp-post-image" alt="" srcset="http://www.neosportsinsiders.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/02/633479580.jpg 594w, http://www.neosportsinsiders.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/02/633479580-300x200.jpg 300w" sizes="(max-width: 594px) 100vw, 594px" /></div>
<p class="entry-meta">
<span class="entry-meta-author vcard author">Posted By: <a class="fn" href="http://www.neosportsinsiders.com/author/mslprod/">Matt Loede</a></span>
<span class="entry-meta-date updated">February 22, 2017</span>
</p>
	<div class="entry-content clearfix">
		<p>NFL contributor Benjamin Allbright has stoked the fires again about the Browns interest in New England Patriots backup QB <strong>Jimmy Garoppolo</strong>, a player who has been linked to the Browns for some time.</p>
<p>Allbright stated in a tweet that it looks to be a two-team race when it comes to the pursuit of <a href="https://www.google.com/url?sa=t&#038;rct=j&#038;q=&#038;esrc=s&#038;source=web&#038;cd=21&#038;cad=rja&#038;uact=8&#038;ved=0ahUKEwjRyZP8j6TSAhVR92MKHb5EAoEQFghjMBQ&#038;url=http%3A%2F%2Fwww.pro-football-reference.com%2Fplayers%2FG%2FGaroJi00.htm&#038;usg=AFQjCNFU-llLlAdYUPPl7xnqA1y0lBUIpA&#038;sig2=AzAZRx3wqNniK9BTDT86kg">Jimmy G</a>, that being not only the Browns, but also the quarterback starved Chicago Bears, who reportedly are <a href="http://www.neosportsinsiders.com/poll-jay-cutler-come-cleveland-browns/">looking to get rid of Jay Cutler</a> as their signal caller.</p>
<p><a href="http://www.neosportsinsiders.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/02/IMG_2249.png"><img class="alignnone  wp-image-17534" src="http://www.neosportsinsiders.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/02/IMG_2249.png" alt="" width="282" height="502" srcset="http://www.neosportsinsiders.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/02/IMG_2249.png 750w, http://www.neosportsinsiders.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/02/IMG_2249-169x300.png 169w, http://www.neosportsinsiders.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/02/IMG_2249-576x1024.png 576w" sizes="(max-width: 282px) 100vw, 282px" /></a></p>
<p>This isn&#8217;t the first time that Allbright has reported that the Browns are very much in the mix for Garoppolo, as <a href="http://www.neosportsinsiders.com/report-browns-becoming-favorite-land-jimmy-garoppolo/">it was back on February 3rd</a> that he stated Cleveland was the front runner to land <a href="https://www.google.com/url?sa=t&#038;rct=j&#038;q=&#038;esrc=s&#038;source=web&#038;cd=8&#038;cad=rja&#038;uact=8&#038;ved=0ahUKEwj2p7W0kKTSAhUE6oMKHcbRACwQFghCMAc&#038;url=http%3A%2F%2Fwww.espn.com%2Fnfl%2Fplayer%2Fstats%2F_%2Fid%2F2330%2Ftom-brady&#038;usg=AFQjCNG7hEU-_7_MDhFxVdH9NYvEsQwMng&#038;sig2=Lk9Zg5xwXBBO6SDEFGuPIA">Tom Brady&#8217;s</a> backup.</p>
<p>There was another piece of information though, <a href="http://www.neosportsinsiders.com/patriots-qb-garoppolo-going-nowhere-one-nfl-insider-says-hes-staying-new-england/">this time by ESPN&#8217;s Ed Werder that stated</a> the Pats are more interested now in keeping Garoppolo long term, possibly having him eventually take over for Brady when he&#8217;s ready to hang up the cleats. </p>
<p><a href="http://bleacherreport.com/articles/2693832-mike-freemans-10-point-stance-patriots-big-trade-bait-nfls-athletic-freaks">Another report today by Bleacher Report&#8217;s Mike Freeman</a> stated that “[Sources] believe several teams are preparing to make blockbuster offers for Garoppolo. … They think a draft shakeup is coming and that it will involve Garoppolo.”</p>
<p>No matter what, it will be an interesting sprint to the NFL Draft if the Pats are serious about moving Garoppolo. </p>
