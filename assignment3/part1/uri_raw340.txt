        <p><h4>Police put value on number 21 jersey at half a million dollars</h4>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><strong>NEW ENGLAND &#8211;</strong> New England Patriots star quarterback Tom Brady reported that the jersey he wore in the Super Bowl 51 last February 5 where he led his football team to a record fifth win has gone missing.</p>
<p>Houston police puts value of the number 12 jersey at US$500,000.</p>
<p>TMZ, an American syndicated entertainment and gossip news show created by Harvey Levin, posted on its website a copy of a police report on the theft of the prized jersey.</p>

<p>Police put value on number 21 jersey at half a million dollars</p>
<p>New England Patriots star quarterback Tom Brady reported that the jersey he wore in the Super Bowl 51 last February 5 where he led his football team to a record fifth win has gone missing.</p>
<p>Houston police puts value of the number 12 jersey at US$500,000.</p>
<p>TMZ, an American syndicated entertainment and gossip news show created by Harvey Levin, posted on its website a copy of a police report on the theft of the prized jersey.</p>
<p>The theft has been classified by the police as first-degree felony.</p>
<p>Brady reported the theft the day after the Superbowl 51.</p>
<p>Brady said after the game, he went to the locker room of the team at the NRG Stadium where the Superbowl was held and put the jersey inside his bag. When he went back to the locker room to get his bag, the jersey was no longer inside.</p>
<p>Brady wants it back because &#8220;its a nice piece of memorabilia.&#8221;</p>
<p>&#8220;If it shows up on (buy-and-sell website) eBay somewhere, someone let me know so I can track that down,&#8221; said Brady, who is the husband of Brazilian supermodel Gisele Bundchen. GAC/Expat Media</p>
