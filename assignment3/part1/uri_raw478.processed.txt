When Do Aging RB's Hit the Fantasy Wall?

   [5]Salute: Pro Football Focus

     In general, running backs backs don’t “fall off a cliff” until their
     age-31 season — not their age-30 season. When discussing running
     backs who see a heavier workload (150 attempts per season), their
     fantasy production drops off during their age-29 season — not their
     age-30 season.
