          <div class="date-posts">
        
<div class='post-outer'>
<div class='post hentry uncustomized-post-template' itemprop='blogPost' itemscope='itemscope' itemtype='http://schema.org/BlogPosting'>

<h3 class='post-title entry-title' itemprop='name'>
Timeless Truths (part 7) - Fighting for that Inch
</h3>
<div class='post-header'>
<div class='post-header-line-1'></div>
</div>
<div class='post-body entry-content' id='post-body-5433282260404888381' itemprop='description articleBody'>
<table align="center" cellpadding="0" cellspacing="0" class="tr-caption-container" style="margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto; text-align: center;"><tbody>
<tr><td style="text-align: center;"><a href="https://1.bp.blogspot.com/-_1_QqIyoQR4/WKipLJWJdFI/AAAAAAAACrw/XJhz9tIv7pojeEe3fCOwFRZfMRxYnh9dACLcB/s1600/NFLgameball.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;"><img border="0" height="304" src="https://1.bp.blogspot.com/-_1_QqIyoQR4/WKipLJWJdFI/AAAAAAAACrw/XJhz9tIv7pojeEe3fCOwFRZfMRxYnh9dACLcB/s320/NFLgameball.jpg" width="320" /></a></td></tr>
<tr><td class="tr-caption" style="text-align: center;"><a href="https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:NFLgameball.jpg" target="_blank"><span style="color: orange;"><i>NFL gameball/wikipedia.com</i></span></a></td></tr>
</tbody></table>
<br />
<b><i><span style="font-size: large;">I have a confession....</span></i></b><br />
<br />
I'm not a sports fan. Especially of football.<br />
<br />
In high school, yes. I played in pep band. Attended most of the games. Rooted for the home team. I knew all the players, the cheerleaders, the mascot, the pep club, and my fellow pep band members.<br />
<br />
After high school, life got in the way. And I didn't pay much attention to sports.<br />
<br />
But while on vacation, going south to the warmer climes, we rendezvoused with friend, who was a sports fan. &nbsp;In the lounge of the motel room we hunkered down to watch the game:<br />
<br />
<a href="https://www.nfl.com/super-bowl" target="_blank"><span style="color: orange; font-size: large;"><b><i>Super Bowl LI</i></b></span></a><br />
<br />
<b><i>Patriots vs. Falcons</i></b><br />
<br />
I enjoy the Super Bowl ads. Usually watch them over and over again on YouTube. &nbsp;I wondered what Lady Gaga would do for half-time. And she did not disappoint her audience.<br />
<br />
First half of the game, the Patriots were getting slaughtered. It looked like it was over.<br />
<br />
During half-time, I wondered what was being said the locker room. &nbsp;All the Falcons had to do was to run out the clock.The Patriots? I thought of Al Pacino's&nbsp;inspirational speech in &nbsp;<a href="http://www.imdb.com/title/tt0146838/" target="_blank"><span style="color: orange;">Any Given Sunday (1999)</span></a>.<br />
<br />
<b><i><span style="font-size: large;">Fighting for that inch.</span></i></b><br />
<br />
<div style="text-align: center;">
(caution: language)</div>
<br />
<div style="text-align: center;">
<iframe allowfullscreen="" frameborder="0" height="315" src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/WO4tIrjBDkk" width="560"></iframe><br />
<span style="font-size: x-small;">( reference:&nbsp;<a href="https://youtu.be/WO4tIrjBDkk" target="_blank"><span style="color: orange;">https://youtu.be/WO4tIrjBDkk</span></a> )</span></div>
<div style="text-align: center;">
<br /></div>
<div style="text-align: left;">
<span style="font-family: inherit;">Maybe the Patriots heard a speech like that.&nbsp;</span><br />
<span style="font-family: inherit;"><br /></span>
<span style="font-family: inherit;"><span style="font-family: inherit;">In the last half of game, a miracle happened.&nbsp;</span>The biggest comeback in Super Bowl history. The game went into over-time. Never happened before.</span><br />
<span style="font-family: inherit;">&nbsp; <br /><span style="font-size: xx-small;"><span style="font-size: small;">The instant playbacks showed the players catching the ball within a inch of the ground, staying in-bounds by that inch, making the goal by an inch.</span></span></span><br />
<span style="font-family: inherit;"><span style="font-size: xx-small;"><span style="font-family: inherit; font-size: small;">&nbsp;&nbsp;</span></span></span><br />
<span style="font-family: inherit;"><span style="font-size: xx-small;"><span style="font-family: inherit; font-size: small;">It was the inches that won the game. A real-time drama that was stranger than fiction.&nbsp;</span></span></span><br />
<span style="font-family: inherit;"><span style="font-size: xx-small;"><span style="font-family: inherit; font-size: small;"><br /></span></span></span>
<span style="font-family: inherit;"><span style="font-size: xx-small;"><span style="font-family: inherit; font-size: small;">Other words....</span></span></span></div>
<div style="text-align: left;">
<span style="font-size: xx-small;"><span style="font-size: small;">&nbsp;&nbsp;</span></span><br />
<span style="font-size: xx-small;"><span style="font-size: large;"><b><i>The Best Game Ever!</i></b></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size: xx-small;"><span style="font-size: large;"><b><i>&nbsp;&nbsp;</i></b></span></span><br />
<span style="font-size: x-small;"><span style="font-size: small;">&nbsp;</span></span><span style="font-size: x-small;"><span style="font-family: inherit; font-size: small;">An inspiration to never give up. And fight for those inches. Even if it's never been done before.</span></span><br />
<span style="font-family: inherit;"><span style="font-size: x-small;"><span style="font-size: small;"><br /></span></span>
That game was a great life lesson. Even made a football fan out of me. And that was a miracle.</span></div>
<div style="text-align: left;">
<span style="font-size: x-small;"><br /></span></div>
