			<div style="text-align: center; " class="freed-top-of-page" data-id="17818" id="freed-2065497216"><a href="http://756c26tnrihhrvz5pepkngo73x.hop.clickbank.net/"><img src="http://i2.wp.com/www.diybikerepair.com/images/banner-468x60.gif?resize=468%2C60" border="0" alt="DIYBikeRepair" data-recalc-dims="1"></a></div><br style="clear: both; display: block; float: none;"><p>New England Patriots: One player to jail, coaches could testify in murder trial<img class="wpe_imgrss" src="http://i1.wp.com/cdnph.upi.com/rss/upi/41d8bd09351cd22b4dfc6e04a1e1f6af/New-England-Patriots-One-player-to-jail-coaches-could-testify-in-murder-trial_t.jpg" data-recalc-dims="1"></p>
<p><span class="story_dl">Feb. 22 (UPI) —</span> It’s an abnormal offseason for the <a href="http://www.upi.com/topic/New_England_Patriots/" title="New England Patriots" class="tpstyle">New England Patriots</a> when one player is headed to jail and coaches might testify in a double-murder trial.</p>
<p><a href="http://www.upi.com/topic/Bill_Belichick/" title="Bill Belichick" class="tpstyle">Bill Belichick</a> and <a href="http://www.upi.com/topic/Josh_McDaniels/" title="Josh McDaniels" class="tpstyle">Josh McDaniels</a> could be involved in <a href="http://www.upi.com/topic/Aaron_Hernandez/" title="Aaron Hernandez" class="tpstyle">Aaron Hernandez</a>‘s double murder case, <a class="tpstyle" href="https://www.bostonglobe.com/metro/2017/02/22/aaron-hernandez-juror-looking-for-something/TicTLL7ZlGb60yixassOgJ/story.html" target="_blank" title="Globe on Patriots">according to the <em>Boston Globe</em></a>. Wide receiver Michael Floyd was recently <a class="tpstyle" href="http://www.upi.com/Sports_News/NFL/2017/02/17/New-England-Patriots-Michael-Floyd-sentenced-to-four-months-in-jail/3241487366954/" target="_blank" title="UPI on Hernandez">sentenced to four months in jail</a> for a December DUI.</p>
<div class="c11"></div>
<p>The former Patriots tight end pleaded not guilty to shooting and killing two men in 2012. He is already serving a life sentence for killing Odin Lloyd in 2013.</p><div style="text-align: center; " class="freed-middle-of-article" data-id="17819" id="freed-211974366"><a href="http://jmunnerlyn.fusionhand.hop.clickbank.net/?prod=1"><img src="http://i2.wp.com/fusionhandles.com/affiliate-resources/images/banners/468x58/B.jpg?resize=468%2C58" alt="fusionhandles.com" data-pagespeed-url-hash="1674710645" onload="pagespeed.CriticalImages.checkImageForCriticality(this);" data-recalc-dims="1"></a></div><br style="clear: both; display: block; float: none;">
<p>Patriots owner <a href="http://www.upi.com/topic/Robert_Kraft/" title="Robert Kraft" class="tpstyle">Robert Kraft</a> testified in the Lloyd trial, while Belichick was also on the witness list.</p>
<p>During his testimony, Kraft told the judge that Hernandez <a class="tpstyle" href="http://www.si.com/nfl/2015/03/31/aaron-hernandez-trial-robert-kraft-testifies" target="_blank" title="SI on Hernandez">told him he was innocent</a>.</p>
<p>Hernandez, 27, is charged with killing Daniel de Abreu and Safiro Furtado in 2012. The duo was killed in an alleged drive-by shooting.</p>
<p>Opening statements for the case are set for March 1. Hernandez played in 38 games for the Patriots, before being released.</p>
