The Concerned Statesmen And Patriots Of Nigeria

   The Concerned Statesmen And Patriots Of Nigeria
   February 22 19:09 2017 [29]Print This Article

   Nigerian group accuse French authorities of sabotaging Nigeria’s war
   against insurgency .

   A coalition of some concerned Nigerian statesmen and Patriots have
   alleged that the French authority has not been sincere with Nigerian
   government in the on going fight against insurgency in the country
   hence total defeat has not been achieved .

   They say overwhelming evidence abounds to attest to this conclusion
   stressing that the insincerity is a ploy to sabotage and undermine all
   the efforts of the Nigerian government to fight the Boko Haram
   insurgents to a stand still .

   Disclosing this today at a press conference in Abuja , the leader of
   the group referred to as the concerned statesmen and patriots of
   Nigeria , professor Emmanuel Ome , on behalf of the group said ,
   “French president Francois Hollande has hosted several summits on Boko
   Haram only for things to get worse . He would have to prove that these
   conferences are not used to make our security and intelligence
   community to share sensitive operational information which in turn are
   handed over to the monsters Boko Haram”.

   The group leader also revealed that ,” the worrisome pattern that Boko
   Haram always kidnap expatriates of French origin each time it is broke
   and the French government will hurry to pay ransom in millions of
   dollars . This is nothing short of creating a legitimate front to pass
   money to Boko Haram in the public glare”.

   The group also queried why there has been no explanation from the
   French authority regarding the sighting of its aircrafts across
   Nigeria’s borders with Cameroon, Chad and Niger on numerous occasions
   by internally displaced people and victims of Boko Haram attacks
   insinuating that “the pattern has been for the terrorists to stage
   spectacular attacks after these sightings ” .

   The group which consists of some academies and Nigerian professionals
   however appealed to the United Nations to intervene and stop any undue
   interference and sabotage by France and other foreign interests
   stressing that ,” the damage done to this point has unleashed
   unthinking Humanitarian crisis that is already a blot on the collective
   human conscience ”
   They also called on the Nigerian government to carry out investigations
   into the roles of France in the execution of the insurgency war in the
   country .
