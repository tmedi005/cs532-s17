Martellus Bennett on Twitter: Don’t tell me to take less money

   02.22.17 at 7:25 pm ET
   By [216]Ryan Hannable

   Over the last 24 hours, Martellus Bennett has been quite active on
   social media.

   On Tuesday night, following a report of Bennett being likely to leave
   New England via free agency, [217]Bennett tweeted he hasn’t even
   thought about being a free agent.

   Then on Wednesday, Bennett appeared upset with people speculating about
   his future and was especially upset with same fans tweeting at him to
   take a pay cut in order to stay with the Patriots.

   [218]Here’s a sampling of the tweets.

     And stop @’ing me about taking less money. You take less money and
     your job? All of you take a pay cut hahaha.

     — Martellus Bennett (@MartysaurusRex) [219]February 22, 2017

     Everyone trying to guess what another man is thinking and projecting
     that into the world is irresponsible.

     — Martellus Bennett (@MartysaurusRex) [220]February 22, 2017

     First of all put some respeck on my name it’s Martellus. And the
     patriots didn’t hand me anything me and my teammates earned that
     shit. [221]https://t.co/mn2Pbol8bg

     — Martellus Bennett (@MartysaurusRex) [222]February 22, 2017
