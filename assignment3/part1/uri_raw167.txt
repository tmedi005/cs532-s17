                    <div class="post_content_am_ngg"><p>As far as hype videos go, this might be the most intimidating one I&#8217;ve ever seen.</p>
<p>Even if you are a New England and Tom Brady hater this Patriots hype video will send chills down your spine.</p>
<blockquote class="twitter-tweet" data-lang="en">
<p dir="ltr" lang="en">&#8220;I offered you&#8230; peace&#8221;</p>
<p>Patriots 2016</p>

<p><script src="//platform.twitter.com/widgets.js" async="" charset="utf-8"></script></p>
<p>The opening monologue clearly hints that the NFL started a war by handing Brady a four-game suspension at the beginning of this season. Now, the four-time Super Bowl champion and Bill Belichick are here to finish the battle.</p>
<p>The ultimate retribution for Brady and all of Pats nation would be for the quarterback to bring home his fifth Super Bowl ring. That starts with a win over the Texans in this Saturday&#8217;s divisional round game.</p>
<p>While Jadeveon Clowney and the Texans defense should cause some problems for the Patriots offense, Brady has looked like a man on a mission this season, throwing for 28 touchdowns and just two interceptions in the 12 games he&#8217;s played.</p>
<p>The only way the Texans may be able to come out with a victory is if Tom Brady gets outed as a Terminator. Since I don&#8217;t see that scenario playing out, be afraid Texans fans be very afraid.</p>
