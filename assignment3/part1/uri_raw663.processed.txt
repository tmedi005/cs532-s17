Operation
Better

   This year, while the world was watching Super Bowl LI, we were making
   it better for the ones who make that day, and every day possible.
   Everything was shot, edited and made ready to broadcast immediately
   following the final moments of Super Bowl LI.

Better, together...

   give the soldiers an in-stadium experience. But they weren't just
   experiencing the game from the stands. They were surrounded by family
   in a Super Bowl LI box suite.

And plush with tech.

   To bridge the distance, we had huggable, high-tech teddy bears in the
   suite equipped with a 360-degree camera that fed the livestream to the
   soldier, and a screen that showed a livestream of the soldier to their
   family.
