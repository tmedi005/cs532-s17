<p>BOSTON (AP) &#8211; The value of Tom Brady&#8217;s missing Super Bowl jersey is listed as $500,000 on a Houston Police Department report.</p>
<p>The New England Patriots quarterback is listed as the complainant in the report dated Feb. 6, the day after the Patriots came back from a 25-point deficit to defeat the Atlanta Falcons 34-28 in overtime. The police report was made public Tuesday.</p>
<p>Investigators have been working to identify who was in the locker room at the time the jersey was taken. A Houston police officer was stationed outside the locker room on the day of the game, but access to the location was controlled by Patriots team security.</p>
<p>The Texas Rangers, the state&#8217;s top law enforcement officers, were assisting in the investigation along with the NFL&#8217;s security department.</p>
