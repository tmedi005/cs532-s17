   By [20]Angella G -

   [21]0

   SAD NEWS! We are sorry to inform you, dear Patriots for a great loss.
   One important woman, professional and one of the favorite Fox News host
   died. Brenda Buttner is not among us any more. She lost the battle with
   the ,,black death” of the 21th century. May she rest in peace!

   Read the details below:

   Brenda Buttner, the host of Fox News Channel’s Bull and Bears, has died
   at age 55 following a battle with cancer.

   Fox News reported that Buttner joined the network in 2000 after blazing
   a trail for female business reporters on CNBC.
   Advertisement - story continues below
     __________________________________________________________________
     __________________________________________________________________

   She was an honors graduate from Harvard University. She went on to
   become a Rhodes scholar, graduating from Oxford University with honors
   in the fields of politics and economics.Buttner was a regular on Fox
   News’ Your World with Neil Cavuto, and the host offered a heartfelt
   tribute to his colleague on Monday’s program.

   “She took stock of life much more than any stock in life,” Cavuto said.
   “It’s what separated her from everyone else in this business. Not just
   dollars, you see, Brenda had depth.”

   The Fox News host said Buttner’s bucket list put most people’s to shame
   because of its focus on others.

   She was incredibly proud of her two daughters, Cavuto noted.

   “Let it be known that Brenda Buttner made us want to watch a business
   show with heart. Her heart, her spirit,” Cavuto said. “She democratized
   dollars and just made sense.”

   Many of her other Fox News colleagues also offered tributes.
