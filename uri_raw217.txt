        <p>SAD NEWS! We are sorry to inform you, dear Patriots for a great loss. One important woman, professional and one of the favorite Fox News host died. Brenda Buttner is not among us any more. She lost the battle with the ,,black death&#8221; of the 21th century. May she rest in peace!</p>
<p>Read the details below:</p>
<p style="padding-left: 30px"><em>Brenda Buttner, the host of Fox News Channel’s Bull and Bears, has died at age 55 following a battle with cancer.</em></p>
<p style="padding-left: 30px"><em>Fox News reported that Buttner joined the network in 2000 after blazing a trail for female business reporters on CNBC.</em></p>
