    </div>

            </figure>
        

        
            <div class="tip-body">
                

                <p><strong>Bacon and guacamole deviled eggs? </strong>Yes, you read right. What's not to love?</p><p><strong>You'll need:</strong></p><ul>
<li>6 hard-boiled eggs</li><li>1 large avocado, mashed</li><li>2 strips bacon, cooked and crumbled</li><li>1 tablespoon minced jalapeno</li><li>1 tablespoon minced red onion</li><li>1 tablespoon minced cilantro</li><li>juice of half a lime</li><li>salt and pepper, to taste</li></ul><p><strong>Here’s how to make them:</strong></p><ul>
<li>Cut the eggs in half and scoop the yolks out into a bowl.</li><li>Mash the yolks with a fork.</li><li>Add the remaining ingredients and mix well.</li><li>Stuff the filling back into the egg white halves.&nbsp;</li></ul>
